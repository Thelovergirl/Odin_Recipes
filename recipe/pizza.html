BBQ Chicken Pizza

Home made BBQ Chicken pizza with a spicy twist.

Its great for a picnic or as a snack. Try it and thank me late

Ingredients

  • 12 inch pre-baked pzza crust
  • 1 cup spicy BBQ sauce
  • 2 sknless boneless chicken breast
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 cupsliced pepperonchini peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Celcius
  2. Place pizza crust o a baking sheet and spread the BBQ sauce
  3. Top with chicken cubes, onions, peppers and cilantro
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Bake for 15 mins
